About the Award Celebration

This celebration recognizes the outstanding students in different fields, including academic excellence, sports achievements, and extracurricular activities. Every year, we honor students who excel in these categories and show dedication to their school life.

The event includes performances, guest speeches, and the grand award ceremony.
